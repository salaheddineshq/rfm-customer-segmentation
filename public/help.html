Getting Started

Welcome to RFM Analytics! This dashboard helps you analyze and manage customer data using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) metrics.

Quick Tour:

  1. Dashboard: View overview statistics and charts
  2. Customers: Search and filter customer database
  3. Products: Track, filter, and view detailed information about all products, including stock, price, revenue, and product images.

How to Search Customers

1

Go to Customers Page

Click "Customers" in the sidebar menu

2

Filter by Segment

Select a customer segment from the dropdown (Champions, Loyal, At Risk, etc.)

3

Search by ID (Optional)

Enter a specific customer ID to find individual customers

4

Click Search

Results will appear in the table below with pagination

Understanding RFM Metrics

R Recency

How recently a customer made a purchase. Lower values are better (recent purchase).

Example: Recency = 5 means last purchase was 5 days ago

F Frequency

How often a customer makes purchases. Higher values are better (frequent buyer).

Example: Frequency = 50 means 50 purchases total

M Monetary Value

Total amount spent by the customer. Higher values are better (high spender).

Example: Monetary = $15,000 total spent

Customer Segments Explained

Champions

Best customers - high recency, frequency, and monetary values. These are your most valuable customers.

Loyal

Regular customers with consistent purchases. Good frequency and monetary values.

Potential Loyalist

Recent customers showing good potential. Worth nurturing into loyal customers.

At Risk

Previously good customers who haven't purchased recently. Need attention to retain.

Need Attention

Below average performance across metrics. Consider special offers or campaigns.

Lost

Haven't purchased in a long time. May require win-back campaigns.

Export Data

You can export customer data to CSV format for external analysis:

  1. Search for customers using filters
  2. Click the "Export" button
  3. File will download automatically
  4. Export includes ALL filtered results (not just current page)

Tip: Export without filters to download all customers in the database.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often is the data updated?

A: Data is updated in real-time. Use the refresh button to reload statistics.

Q: How many customers can I view per page?

A: The table displays 10 customers per page. Use pagination to browse through results.

Q: Can I search for multiple customers at once?

A: Currently, search one customer ID at a time. Use segment filters to find groups of customers.

Q: What chart types are available?

A: Dashboard features a doughnut chart (segment distribution) and bar chart (RFM metrics by segment).

Q: How do I clear my search filters?

A: Click the "Clear" button to reset all filters and reload all customers.
